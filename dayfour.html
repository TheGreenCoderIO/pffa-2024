RAW! UNCUT! VIDEO! | 21:00 | Filmhuis Cavia

This is the story of the legendary Palm Drive Video, a kinky gay porn studio run by Jack Fritscher and Mark Hemry. When the AIDS epidemic swept through San Francisco, this couple moved into a ranch in North-California and set up a home porn studio where they made a tremendous and wild array of safe-sex kinky porn tapes during the 1980s. This film is both touching and terribly amusing at times, mixing interviews with the protagonists and footage from their vast film archive. Palm Drive Video porn tapes are unique in their use of non-professional performers and the intense fetish fantasies they depict. In Raw! Uncut! Video! we discover the story of the thoughtful couple behind these films and admire the unrepeatable creative freedom they found in a faraway ranch.

NEEDLE PLAY workshop | 13:00 | PIC

No previous experience necessary.

Puncture into the domain of needle play with Kiki Kazimi and Rick Jansen. This workshop is specifically for individuals with no prior experience in needle play but are keen to try, as a “poker” and/or “pincushion”. I.e. as someone doing the piercing and/or receiving it). Kiki and Rick will share the medical evolution and navigation within alternative sexual practices, providing you with the essential knowledge to safely incorporate needles into your play. The workshop covers crucial topics such as safety, hygiene, anatomy, materials and supplies, basic needle play techniques, emergency procedures, and aftercare. Under supervision, participants can delve into hands-on practice, learning and experimenting on a partner or themselves.
