ACCESSIBILITY

Filmhuis Cavia is on the first floor and since the building has no elevator, it is unfortunately not easily accessible for people with reduced mobility due to two staircases. Contact us if there’s anything we can do to help you access the screenings.

Filmtheater de Uitkijk is on the ground floor and is accessible for people with reduced mobility, the screening room itself might be too narrow for wider wheelchairs to pass. Please send us an email or a message beforehand so we can figure out together how we can best help you to join the screenings.

The Prostitution Information Center is on the ground floor but there are three steps right before the entrance. Please send us an email or a message beforehand so we can figure out together how we can best help you to join the workshops.

The Clifford Studio is on the first floor with stairs, and is therefore unfortunately not easily accessible for people with reduced mobility.
